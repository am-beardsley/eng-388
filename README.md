# eng-388
<p>ENG 388: Writing for the Web is an upper-level undergraduate course at Western Illinois University.
Our course explores the questions including but not limited to: <ul>
  <li>What's your favoriate food?</li>
  <li>Where does it originate?</li>
  <li>What is the most popular food on campus? </li></ul>

Dr. Beardsley maintains the project, and students in the course contribute.
